## How it works

**Section heading:** How your A-team gets built
**Section subhead:** Three habits, a few minutes each. The app does the remembering.

**Step 1 — Invite people you trust**
Your roster is invite-only. No strangers, no applicants — just the part-timers
you'd hire again tomorrow.

**Step 2 — Let your crew raise their hands**
Post a shift and watch interest roll in. Confirm the right person in one tap —
or assign manually, your call.

**Step 3 — Recognize great work**
Send kudos after a shift, and quietly note who showed up strong. Recognition
compounds — so does reliability.

## Objection-handling section (optional, add if page needs length)

**Heading:** Not another gig app

**"Is this a marketplace?"** No. Nobody can browse your crew and your crew
can't be poached. People join only when you invite them.

**"Do my part-timers get rated?"** Never publicly. You keep private notes on
attendance and quality — they see kudos and milestones. Recognition is loud,
records are quiet.

**"What if someone turns down a shift?"** Nothing happens. Declining is free by
design — the fastest way to lose good people is to punish them for having a life.

**"Does it handle payroll?"** It tracks hours and what's owed, and you mark
paid when you've paid — however you pay. No processing, no fees on wages.
